      Chicago Artist Files (CAF) is a vertical file documenting more than 100 years of Chicago art history, free and accessible to the public at Chicago Public Library's Harold Washington Library Center. The files and exhibits by local artists began in the 1920s and were most assiduously developed from the mid-1940s through today. CAF includes bios, artist statements, resumes, CVs, exhibition notices and postcards, press releases, articles, gallery flyers and exhibition catalogs.
